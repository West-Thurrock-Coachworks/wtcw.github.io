About Us

Founded in 1977, West Thurrock Coachworks started the business from modest roots and over the years expanded into two main divisions serving the needs of private car and heavy commercial trucks and coaches. By continually keeping pace with technology using state of the art equipment and processes, WTC has achieved an excellent reputation for consistent high standards on body repairs and paintwork finishes. The success of the company is not taken for granted and our focus is always on our customers be it Fleet or Private customers. Our customers and clients are the main reason behind our success and a relationship WTC are extremely proud of and reflected in the continued growth and stability of the company through recommendation and return business.
